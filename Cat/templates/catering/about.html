About Us

Professional catering services with a passion for exceptional food and service

Our Story

For over 25years Trade Bells Services (TBS) has been a leading catering company in Nairobi specialising in providing corporate catering, institutional catering as well as catering to events and private functions.

Our professionally trained staff are highly creative and use only the freshest produce available in creating the perfect blend of mouth watering & nutritious meals.

We pride ourselves in our personal approach to catering to our esteemed clients’ needs through an emphasis on sound business ethics, quality services & delivering value for money

To this regard, TBS is a certified company that is compliant with all mandatory regulatory & statutory requirements.
